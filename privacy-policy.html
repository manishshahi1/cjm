PRIVACY POLICY

This Privacy Policy explains how Click Joy Media ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information in connection with the Diverse Vertical Affiliate Initiative Program ("Program"). This policy applies to all users of the Program, including affiliates, merchants, and program participants.

1. Information We Collect

We collect a variety of information from you, depending on your role in the Program:

  • Affiliates:
    1. Personal information: Name, email address, phone number, company name, website URL, social media handles.
    2. Demographic information: Age, gender, location.
    3. Performance data: Traffic, leads, conversions, sales.
    4. Payment information: Payment method, account details (if applicable).
  • Merchants:
    1. Company information: Name, business address, contact information, website URL.
    2. Product or service information: Descriptions, prices, images, marketing materials.
    3. Performance data: Sales, click-through rates, conversion rates.
    4. Program Participants:
    5. Personal information: Name, email address, location (if relevant to the Program).
    6. Engagement data: Participation in Program activities, surveys, feedback.


2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Manage the Program:
    1. Verify your eligibility and participation in the Program.
    2. Track performance and provide marketing support.
    3. Facilitate communication between affiliates, merchants, and program participants.
    4. Send program updates and announcements.
    5. Analyze and improve the Program:
    6. Understand user behavior and preferences.
    7. Develop and improve Program features and functionality.
    8. Measure the effectiveness of the Program.
  • Fulfill legal obligations:
    1. Comply with applicable laws and regulations.
    2. Resolve disputes and enforce our agreements.


3. Your Information Choices

You have certain choices regarding your information:

  • Access and correction: You may request access to and correction of your personal information by contacting us through the methods provided below.
  • Marketing communications: You may opt out of receiving marketing communications from us by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in any of our emails or contacting us through the methods provided below.

  • Law enforcement agencies and other third parties: We may disclose your information to law enforcement agencies or other third parties if we believe it is necessary to comply with the law, protect our rights or the rights of others, or prevent fraud or abuse.


5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

6. International Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. These countries may have different data protection laws than your own country. We will take appropriate steps to ensure that your information is treated responsibly and in accordance with applicable laws.

7. Children's Privacy

The Program is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.
